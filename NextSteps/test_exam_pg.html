Entrance Exams

Entrance exams, for admissions to various B.E./B.Tech courses, conduted by various states, universities and colleges in India.
Entrance Exam Description Exam Level State Applies to
ACET EEE
Amritsar College of Engineering and Technology conducts ACET EEE for admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes. College
Amity JEE
Admission to Amity University B.Tech programs are based on the Amity Joint Entrance Examination (AmityJEE). Amity JEE is a Computer Based Test (CBT) conducted by Pearson VUE (for Amity) and is held in various cities across India and abroad. University
AMUEEE
Aligarh Muslim University Engineering Entrance Exam - for admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes. University
AP EAMCET
Andhra Pradesh - Engineering Agricultural and Medical Common Entrance Test,(EAMCET) is conducted for admission to some engineering and medical colleges in the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana State Andhra Pradesh
ASSAM CEE
Assam Combined Entrance Examination is a state-government controlled centralized test, conducted by the Assam Science and Technology University for admission to many private and governmental engineering institutions in Assam. State Assam
AUEET
Andhra University, Vishakhapatnam conducts AUEET (Andhra University Engineering Entrance Test) For Admission into Engineering Courses of Andhra University and its Affiliated Colleges University
BCECE
Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) is constituted under Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Act, 1995 . It conducts competitive examinations every year for admissions in various professional courses of Medical, Engineering and Agricultural streams in the Institutions of the state of Bihar. State Bihar
BITSAT
BITSAT is a Computer based online test for Admissions to Integrated First Degree Programmes of Birla Institute of Technology And Science (BITS) Campuses in Pilani, Goa and Hyderabad. University
BPTPIA-CET
Bihar Private Technical & Professional Institutions Association (BPTPIA) conducts the competitive entrance test (CET) for candidates seeking admission into under graduate (UG) Engineering/Technology Degree (B.Tech) and Diploma (Polytechnic) courses. State Bihar
BVP-CET
Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University, Pune conducts Bharati Vidyapeeth Common Entrance Test (BVP CET) every year for admissions to various B.Tech programmes to 700 B. Tech seats offered by the university. BVP CET is a university level entrance exam which is conducted in online mode University
CENTAC
CENTAC (Centralized Admission Committee) is the Counselling Agency constituted by the Government of Puducherry to do counselling for admissions to various Post graduate and undergraduate courses in the UT of Puducherry. Union Territory Puducherry
CG PET
Chhattisgarh Pre Engineering Test or CG PET is a state level engineering entrance exam conducted every year by Directorate of Technical Education, Chhattisgarh, for admissions to undergraduate engineering courses available across the participating institutes of CG PET in the state of Chhattisgarh. State Chhattisgarh
COMEDK
Consortium of Medical Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMED K) conducts a state-level examination called COMEDK UGET (Undergraduate Entrance Test) to offer admissions to engineering and medical degree programmes offered by institutes that are affiliated to COMEDK. State Karnataka
GCET
Goa CET is a state level entrance exam conducted by Directorate of Tehnical Education (DTE), Goa, for admmissions to prefessional degree programmes in engineering and pharmacy. State Goa
GUJCET
GUJCET is conducted by Gujarat Secpndary Education Board for admissions to undergraduate engineering and pharmacy courses offered by various institutes in Gujarat. State Gujarat
IPU-CET
Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test (IPU CET) is a university-level undergraduate engineering entrance exam conducted by Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU) University
JCECE
Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board was constituted under Section 85 of Bihar Re-Constituted Act, 2000 by the Govt. vide Memo No. 374 dated 29.03.2001. The Government of Jharkhand has assigned the responsibility of conducting entrance tests for admission into various engineering Institutions in the state. State Jharkhand
JEE (ADV)
JEE Advacend is conducted by one of IITs under the guidance of Joint Admission Board(JAB) to offer admissions to IITs. National
JEE (MAIN)
Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) MAIN will be conducted by the NTA from 2019 onwards. This Examination was being conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) till 2018. JEE Main is applicable for admission to NITs, IIITs and CFTIs participating though Central Seat Allocation Board subject to the condition that the candidate should have secured at least 75% marks in the 12th class examination, or be in the top 20 percentile in the 12th class examination conducted by the respective Boards. For SC/ST candidates the qualifying marks would be 65% in the 12th class examination. National
JKCET
THE J& K Board of Professional Entrance Examinations conducts the Common Entrance Test for B.E./B. Tech. (Engineering) Courses at various centres. in Jammu and Srinagar State Jammu and Kashmir
KCET
Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) conducts Common Entrance Test (CET) called KCET for admissions to professional engineering programmes in institiions in Karnataka State Karnataka
KEAM
Kerala Engineering, Architecture and Medical and Pharmacy Courses. State Kerala
KEE
Karunya University conducts Karunya Engineering Entrance Examination (KEE) to offer admissions to it's undergraduate engineering programmes. University
KIITEE
The KIIT Entrance Examination (KIITEE) is conducted by KIIT University for admissions for undergraduate engineering programmes University
KLUEEE
The Koneru Lakshmaiah Education Foundation (KL University) conducts KLU Engineering Entrance Exam (KLUEEE) for admission to it's B.tech programmes. University
KRLMPCA-UGCET
The Karnataka Religious & Linguistic Mionority Colleges Association, Bangalore is functioning since January 2004 is catering to the needs of candidates who aspire for Medical, Dental and Engineering with particular reference to Minority candidates of Karnataka. This Association is having 3 Medical Colleges, 5 Dental Colleges and 9 Engineering Colleges as its members. The Association conducts UG CET Entrance Test for filling up seats in B.E. course available with its' member institutions. State Karnataka
KRLMPCA CET
KRLMPCA (Karnataka Religious & Linguistic Minority Professional Colleges Association CET) conducts CET(Common Entrance Test) for admissions to BE programmes offered by some of colleges in Karnataka. State Karnataka
MET
Manipal Entrance Test (MET) - Conducted by Manipal University for undergraduate engineering programme admissions University
MHCET
Commmon Entrance Test for institutes in Maharashtra. State Maharashtra
MP-PET
====Not active====== Replaced by JEE State Madhya Pradesh
NATA
National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) is being conducted by COA since 2006 National
OJEE
Odisha Joint Entrance Examination Board (OJEEB) conducts OJEE for admissions to undergrad engineering programmes in private and government engineering colleges in Odisha. State Odisha
PESSAT
PESSAT is an entrance exam conducted by PES University, Bangalore, offering admission to the university programmes University
REAP
Rajasthan engineering Admission Process (REAP) is a process for admission in B.Tech/B.Arch courses in institutes affiliated to RTU/BTU, MBM Engineering College Jodhpur, CTAE Engineering College, Udaipur. State Rajasthan
SAT
SAT is conducted by USA based College Board and lot of Institutes offering international admissions have SAT test scores as basis for merit list. International
SAT SUBJECT TESTS
SAT Subject Test is conducted by USA based College Board and lot of Institutes offering international admissions have SAT Subjects Test scores as basis for merit list. International
SRMJEE(UG)
Sri Ramaswami Memorial University (SRM) conducts the SRM Joint Engineering Entrance Examination - SRMJEEE for the B.Tech seats in the University. University
TANCET
TAMIL NADU COMMON ENTRANCE TEST conducted by Anna University, Chennai. The Government of Tamil Nadu have authorized Anna University for conducting the exam. State Tamil Nadu
TNEA
Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) is conducted by Anna University for admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes at various engineering institutes in the state. State Tamil Nadu
TNPCEE
Tamil Nadu Professional Courses Entrance Examinations (TNPCEE) is an entry level test conducted by Anna University, Chennai on behalf of the Government of Tamil Nadu for candidates who seek admission to all Professional Courses in Tamil Nadu. Besides Anna University there are many participating institutes of TNPCEE. The rank of the students is determined on the basis of performance of the candidate in the TNPCEE and Class 12 Board Examination. The TNPCEE carries 100 marks whereas the Class 12 Board Examination carries 200 marks (marks secured in Class 12 Board Exam of Mathematics, Physics and Chemistry normalized to 100 marks, 50 marks and 50 marks respectively). State Tamil Nadu
TS EAMCET
Telengana State - Engineering Agricultural and Medical Common Entrance Test,(EAMCET) is conducted for admission to some engineering and medical colleges in the Andhra Pradesh and Telangana State Telangana
UKSEE
Uttarakhand Technical University conducts UKSEE(Uttarakhand State Entrance Exam) for admissions to engineering and other programmes offered by the University and other Departments and Government/Private Institutes located in the state. State Uttarakhand
UPSEE
The entrance examination for undergraduate level engineering institutions and other professional colleges in Uttar Pradesh. It is conducted by Dr A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow. State Uttar Pradesh
VITEEE
Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) conducts Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Exam (VITEEE) to offer admissions to B.Tech. at its various campuses. University
VSAT
VSAT is an entrnace exam conduted by Vignan University offering admissions to undergraduate engineering programmes University
WBJEE
The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) conducts Joint Entrance Examination for admission to various institutions in West Bengal imparting education in medical, engineering and technological courses. State West Bengal
