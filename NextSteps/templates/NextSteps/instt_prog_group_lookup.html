Search Programs by General Name


Various institutes use slightly different names for same or similar programs. You can use this feature to enter a general name for the program you are interested in and we will display all the programs with similar names and intstitutes offering those. You can then copy the program or Institute your are interested in and use those to search in "Set Preferences" and "JEE Seat Chances" features.

For an example, for searching all the diffirent programs in Cumputer Science, just enter "Computer Science". We will display all the programs that begin with Computer Science.
