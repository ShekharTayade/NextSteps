Overview - Admission Assistant Features


Admission Calendar Never miss important dates (that can cost dearly!). A calendar that shows important dates for the preferred (shortlisted) institutess. This helps you manage and track all the dates and get the reminded of. You can also print out a to-do list
JEE Seat Chances Highly useful tool to check your chances of a seat through JEE. Based on anticipated JEE rank and previous year opening/closing ranks, find out the probability of getting a seat in chosen institutes, program. Play out an if-then-analysis using this tool
Study Planner Powerful graphical dashboard to know where you are with respect to your plan. With this feature you can plan, track and manage your study hours and get a graphical dashboard.
Compare Institute Compare various institutes on various parameter. This features helps you to search institutes, select them and compare.
View/Set Preferences View and set the program, institutes preferences (shortlist institutes that you want to target). This features helps you customize other NextSteps features so as to work with your preferred institutes.)

All these search results further let you narrow your search so you find precisely what you are looking for.
