NextSteps.co.in - User Guide

While you study hard to secure admission to your preferred Institute, you need all the help you can get on the admission processes, important information, dates, assessment of your chances, maintain a calendar of when-to-do-what and more. Missing out on an important piece of information or a date may mean missing out on a great chance. NextSteps helps with the features and tools that give you information, data and more. It will save your precious time and effort and help you organize yourself, manage the admission process better.

Search Features

  • Search institutes across India with detailed information on the institutes
  • Details on institutes entrance exams, programs offered, survey ranks, JEE opening/closing ranks etc.
  • Consolidated information on your preferred institutes

“Admission Assistant” Features and Tools

  • Excellent admission calendar – keep track of all the important dates, customize it yourself
  • Assess your chances through JEE – gives you possibility of securing a seat through JEE
  • Powerful Study Planner – An advanced tool to manage your study hours. Has a graphical dashboard to show where you stand w.r.t. your plan
  • Compare Institutes – A tool you can use to compare various institutes on various parameters
  • Record Application Details – A handy tool to record all the details you need while applying to various college. Helps you save time and effort.
NextSteps.co.in is a website dedicated to helping students who are looking forward to undergraduate engineering college admission. NextSteps has a database of over 3000 undergraduate engineering colleges across India. It is “Your Online Admission Assistant”.

Access to NextSteps

You can search institute details even without sign up. Just go to https://www.NextSteps.co.in and start searching institutes. It is free.
Access to all other “Search” options on the website is free. You just sign up and start using. Once signed up, you can search Institutes/Colleges by programs/courses, location, type of Institute, get information on programs, seats and quotas, survey ranking, opening & closing JEE ranks, and cut offs for various Institutes. These are essential ingredients in looking for colleges that are best fit for you. This section can be accessed from the home page under Search section. You can use a range of more useful Admission Assisting features, and tools as you subscribe to NextSteps. These help save a lot of time and effort so you can concentrate on your studies.
