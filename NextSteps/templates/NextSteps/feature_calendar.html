As you prepare to apply to institutes that you are interested in, the Calendar informs you of all the important dates and actions you needs to take for those institute admissions. During the admission process season you would get really busy with numerous applications, and exams. So keeping track of things to do, dates, actions is quite a task. You probably are aware of and might have heard stories of how costly missing a date can be. This tool can help you.

NextSteps maintains a database of the important exams and admission process dates. The calendar automatically gets generated for your preferred institutes. You can add, remove, edit events from the calendar as you wish. You can generate a "To-Do" list from this calendar too.

  • Add an event: example-  you want to make sure that online payment is done by a specific date or all documentations is in place, or even that you wish to keep track of special classes that you want to attend
  • Remove event(s) - When you don't want keep track of a specific event you can just select and delete it
  • Edit event(s) : You change the dates of an event by simplying dragging it
  • To-Do List : A list of the events from calndar in tabular format
