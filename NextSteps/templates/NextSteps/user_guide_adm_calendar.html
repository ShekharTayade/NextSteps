Admission Calendar

Keep track of important exam, admission process dates, get a to-do list
As you prepare to apply to institutes that you are interested in, the Admission Calendar informs you of all the important dates and actions you need to take.
  • On the home page, click on “Admission Calendar” under NextSteps Features section.
  • Based on the preferred insititutes and availability of data calendar shows you the important dates such as application open/close, entrance exam date, results date etc.
  • When you access the calendar for the first time, NextSteps fetches the importants for your preferred institutes. You can then change those dates, add your own and remove the ones you don't want to track. Once make any changes, please save your changes using using . Those are then retained in your calendar.
  • An event can span over multiple dates
  • Navigate the month and year using the buttons at the top
  • Important date in a month will show up as an event on a particular date. Keep your cursor on the event and a popup will show name of the institute and complete details of the event.
  • Clicking an event toggles between selecting and unslecting it
    • Changes by Dragging Events
    • You can click-and-drag an event to another date
    • you can extend or shrink an event by draging it's edge

Add Your Own Event

  • To add your own event click on the
  • A small window will popup where you can enter the details of the event - Institutes Name, Event, Start and End Dates. Leave End Date empty if its only one day event.
  • Click on Create button to show this new event on the calendar or close the pop up window to cancel.
  • Don't forget to save the calendar using . Your changes are not saved permanently until you save.

Remove Event(s)

  • To remove/delete an event click on the event to select it
  • Make sure the event to be removed is selected (it's background color will become red when selected) and then click on
  • Selected event will be immediately removed
  • To remove multiple events, select all to be deleted and click on
  • Don't forget to save the calendar using . Your changes are not saved permanently until you save.

Remove All Events

Use this when you basically want to start a fresh. Having made some changes you may want to revert to the important dates that NextSteps has in it;s database. So you can remove all the events and then re-fetch (click on rest calendar ) from the NextSteps database so that the important dates for your preferred institutes are restored on your calendar.

  • To remove all the events from you calendar click on
  • All event will be immediately removed
  • Don't forget to save the calendar using . You changes are not saved permanently until you save.

Reset Calendar

  • To Reset your calendar (fetch the dates again from NextSteps database), click on
  • All your current event will be removed and calendar will be populated with the events (important dates) as available for your preferred institutes from the NextSteps database.
  • Don't forget to save the calendar using . You changes are not saved permanently until you save.

To-Do List

  • Click on to display all events in your calendar in format of a to-do list.
  • The Search box at the top is free form search. The text you enter here will be searched in all the columns displayed. For an example to dispaly only the events/dates for institutes with name containing Bombay, just enter Bombay.
  • This list can be exported in Excel or PDF format or printed on the printer connnected to your computer.
  • You can sort in ascending or descending order by clicking on the headers of the columns.
  • You can also remove event using this page. Select a row by clickin it and then click on "Delete Selected Row" button. If you are done with a particular event you can use this option.
